<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN" style="font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;
font-family:&quot;Calibri&quot;,sans-serif">I was in Chennai for a Visa interview on a
Friday morning and then had the rest of the day, plus the weekend to myself. I
had to be back in Bangalore by Sunday night, and hence could not travel very
far. Mahabalipuram was an obvious choice - it’s ideally located 1.5 hours from
Chennai, is a UNESCO World Heritage site and has vibrant beach cafes. I needed
no other reasons to head to Mahabalipuram, though I did end up discovering
more!</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN" style="font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;
font-family:&quot;Calibri&quot;,sans-serif">Whenever I travel solo, I prefer not to book
a hotel beforehand so that I can explore the area and find the most suited one,
keeping in mind:</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left:36.0pt;text-indent:-18.0pt"><span lang="EN" style="font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:&quot;Calibri&quot;,sans-serif">-<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span><span lang="EN" style="font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:&quot;Calibri&quot;,sans-serif">Proximity
to the spots I want to visit, since I travel on foot when alone</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left:36.0pt;text-indent:-18.0pt"><span lang="EN" style="font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:&quot;Calibri&quot;,sans-serif">-<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span><span lang="EN" style="font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:&quot;Calibri&quot;,sans-serif">Security,
since I definitely don’t want to end up in a suspicious neighbourhood</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left:36.0pt;text-indent:-18.0pt"><span lang="EN" style="font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:&quot;Calibri&quot;,sans-serif">-<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span><span lang="EN" style="font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:&quot;Calibri&quot;,sans-serif">Access
to the bus / train station (either close-by or having some readily available
means of transportation)</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN" style="font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;
font-family:&quot;Calibri&quot;,sans-serif">&nbsp;</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN" style="font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;
font-family:&quot;Calibri&quot;,sans-serif">Hence, on reaching Mahabalipuram, I deboarded
the cab at one end of the beach (at the Shore Temple) and went hotel-hopping on
foot. I realised that the main part of Mahabalipuram Beach was extremely
crowded, and hence went in the opposite direction (north). There were a couple
of resorts (albeit shabby), costing ~Rs. 5K per room per night. Since I didn’t
want to spend that much, I went to the hotels next to them. I felt Seashore,
Santana and Kalo’s had decent rooms - and chose to stay at Seashore. It had a modest
room with a beautiful sea-facing view and a rooftop cafe, costing ~Rs. 2K per
night. If luxury is what you are looking for, you have Radisson, Welcome Group,
Taj resorts as well, all further north.</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN" style="font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;
font-family:&quot;Calibri&quot;,sans-serif">&nbsp;</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="border:none"><b><span lang="EN" style="font-size:12.0pt;

Mahabalipuram - Itinerary</span></b></p>

<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN" style="font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;

<p class="MsoNormal"><b><span lang="EN" style="font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;
font-family:&quot;Calibri&quot;,sans-serif">Day 1: Chilling at beach cafes, swimming</span></b></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left:36.0pt;text-indent:-18.0pt"><span lang="EN" style="font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:&quot;Calibri&quot;,sans-serif">-<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span><span lang="EN" style="font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:&quot;Calibri&quot;,sans-serif">I
checked-in to my hotel around 1 pm</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left:36.0pt;text-indent:-18.0pt"><span lang="EN" style="font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:&quot;Calibri&quot;,sans-serif">-<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span><span lang="EN" style="font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:&quot;Calibri&quot;,sans-serif">Being
March, the Sun was bright, and hence the first thing I did was to take a
shower!</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left:36.0pt;text-indent:-18.0pt"><span lang="EN" style="font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:&quot;Calibri&quot;,sans-serif">-<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span><span lang="EN" style="font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:&quot;Calibri&quot;,sans-serif">I
headed straight to the rooftop cafe, ordered some food and drinks and spent the
afternoon with a book and some solemn music</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left:72.0pt;text-indent:-18.0pt"><span lang="EN" style="font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:&quot;Calibri&quot;,sans-serif">○<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span><span lang="EN" style="font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:&quot;Calibri&quot;,sans-serif">A
beautiful sea, a sparse beach (being a Friday), soothing wind, a pretty cafe,
an intellectually stimulating book and slow music. What more could I have asked
for!</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left:72.0pt;text-indent:-18.0pt;border:none"><span lang="EN" style="font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:&quot;Calibri&quot;,sans-serif">○<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span><span lang="EN" style="font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:&quot;Calibri&quot;,sans-serif">Here’s
what more I could have asked for: less Sun and a more beautiful beach! The
beach had some litter and many fishing boats anchored haphazardly. That said, I
think it was definitely better than most Indian beaches - the water was clean,
land not as dirty and good crowd (less in quantity, sensible in quality)</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" align="center" style="text-align:center"><span lang="EN" style="font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:&quot;Calibri&quot;,sans-serif"><img width="79" height="129" id="image8.png" src="Mahabalipuram%20(3%20days,%202%20nights%20_%20Solo).fld/image002.jpg"></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left:36.0pt;text-indent:-18.0pt"><span lang="EN" style="font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:&quot;Calibri&quot;,sans-serif">-<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span><span lang="EN" style="font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:&quot;Calibri&quot;,sans-serif">As
the Sun set, I decided to go in for a swim</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left:72.0pt;text-indent:-18.0pt;border:none"><span lang="EN" style="font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:&quot;Calibri&quot;,sans-serif">○<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span><span lang="EN" style="font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:&quot;Calibri&quot;,sans-serif">I
like to just lay in water. Try this whenever you are in water next (sea or a
pool) and if you know swimming - float on your back with your ears underwater.
You are able to hear the water and it cancels the rest of the background noise.
It’s one of the most calming and relaxing sensations I have experienced!</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left:36.0pt;text-indent:-18.0pt"><span lang="EN" style="font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:&quot;Calibri&quot;,sans-serif">-<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span><span lang="EN" style="font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:&quot;Calibri&quot;,sans-serif">Late
evening, I went to Radisson for dinner</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left:72.0pt;text-indent:-18.0pt;border:none"><span lang="EN" style="font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:&quot;Calibri&quot;,sans-serif">○<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span><span lang="EN" style="font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:&quot;Calibri&quot;,sans-serif">It’s
a beautiful property. They have a gorgeous restaurant by the beach (which is
~0.5 kms from the entrance! Hence they have golf carts to ferry people)</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left:72.0pt;text-indent:-18.0pt;border:none"><span lang="EN" style="font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:&quot;Calibri&quot;,sans-serif">○<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span><span lang="EN" style="font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:&quot;Calibri&quot;,sans-serif">I
spent about 5 hours there, again in the company of my book and some good
African music, slowly eating a delicious vegetarian Lasagna and sipping my drink</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" align="center" style="text-align:center"><span lang="EN" style="font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:&quot;Calibri&quot;,sans-serif"><img width="181" height="198" id="image13.png" src="Mahabalipuram%20(3%20days,%202%20nights%20_%20Solo).fld/image003.jpg"></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left:36.0pt;text-indent:-18.0pt"><span lang="EN" style="font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:&quot;Calibri&quot;,sans-serif">-<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span><span lang="EN" style="font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:&quot;Calibri&quot;,sans-serif">I
walked back, all set for the sunrise the next day</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN" style="font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;
font-family:&quot;Calibri&quot;,sans-serif">&nbsp;</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal"><b><span lang="EN" style="font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;
font-family:&quot;Calibri&quot;,sans-serif">Day 2: Exploring the monuments</span></b></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left:36.0pt;text-indent:-18.0pt"><span lang="EN" style="font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:&quot;Calibri&quot;,sans-serif">-<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span><span lang="EN" style="font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:&quot;Calibri&quot;,sans-serif">The
Sun rises at ~5:45 am (in March beginning). I got up in time to see it rise
through my window. Luckily, the sky was kind enough to be clear that day</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" align="center" style="text-align:center"><span lang="EN" style="font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:&quot;Calibri&quot;,sans-serif"><img width="248" height="186" id="image6.png" src="Mahabalipuram%20(3%20days,%202%20nights%20_%20Solo).fld/image004.jpg"></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left:36.0pt;text-indent:-18.0pt"><span lang="EN" style="font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:&quot;Calibri&quot;,sans-serif">-<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span><span lang="EN" style="font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:&quot;Calibri&quot;,sans-serif">When
I finally woke up for good, I was starving. Sri Anand Bhavan happened to be
close-by and a recommended restaurant for a heavy vegetarian meal</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left:36.0pt;text-indent:-18.0pt"><span lang="EN" style="font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:&quot;Calibri&quot;,sans-serif">-<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span><span lang="EN" style="font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:&quot;Calibri&quot;,sans-serif">Around
1 pm, I headed to the main attraction of Mahabalipuram - the famous
archaeological monuments</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left:72.0pt;text-indent:-18.0pt;border:none"><span lang="EN" style="font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:&quot;Calibri&quot;,sans-serif">○<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span><span lang="EN" style="font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:&quot;Calibri&quot;,sans-serif">There
are three separate complexes with some monuments within each</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left:72.0pt;text-indent:-18.0pt;border:none"><span lang="EN" style="font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:&quot;Calibri&quot;,sans-serif">○<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span><span lang="EN" style="font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:&quot;Calibri&quot;,sans-serif">A
single ticket (Rs. 30 for Indians; Rs. 500 for foreigners) allows you entry to
all three complexes</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal"><u><span lang="EN" style="font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;
font-family:&quot;Calibri&quot;,sans-serif">Itinerary for the monument visit:<br>
<br>
</span></u></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left:36.0pt;text-indent:-18.0pt"><span lang="EN" style="font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:&quot;Calibri&quot;,sans-serif">-<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span><span lang="EN" style="font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:&quot;Calibri&quot;,sans-serif">I
planned to visit the largest complex first, then the Pancha Rathas and finally
the Shore Temple, which is by the sea, hence offering a great view to relax
after the tiring day</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left:36.0pt;text-indent:-18.0pt"><span lang="EN" style="font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:&quot;Calibri&quot;,sans-serif">-<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span><span lang="EN" style="font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:&quot;Calibri&quot;,sans-serif">Largest
complex (1.5-2 hours):</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left:72.0pt;text-indent:-18.0pt;border:none"><span lang="EN" style="font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:&quot;Calibri&quot;,sans-serif">○<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span><span lang="EN" style="font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:&quot;Calibri&quot;,sans-serif">I
entered from Krishna’s Butterball side and exited from the Lighthouse side,
which is closer to the second complex</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left:72.0pt;text-indent:-18.0pt;border:none"><span lang="EN" style="font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:&quot;Calibri&quot;,sans-serif">○<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span><span lang="EN" style="font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:&quot;Calibri&quot;,sans-serif">There
are several excavated temples and structures, and thankfully, direction-signs
to guide you</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left:72.0pt;text-indent:-18.0pt;border:none"><span lang="EN" style="font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:&quot;Calibri&quot;,sans-serif">○<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span><span lang="EN" style="font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:&quot;Calibri&quot;,sans-serif">But
still, I felt there was a need to follow Google Maps, so as to not miss any
site. For eg. Arjuna’s Penance and Krishna Mandapa are accessible from the road
and not from within the complex</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left:72.0pt;text-indent:-18.0pt;border:none"><span lang="EN" style="font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:&quot;Calibri&quot;,sans-serif">○<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span><u><span lang="EN" style="font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:&quot;Calibri&quot;,sans-serif;
color:#6AA84F">Highlights:</span></u><span lang="EN" style="font-size:12.0pt;
line-height:115%;font-family:&quot;Calibri&quot;,sans-serif"> I liked Krishna’s
Butterball, Arjuna’s Penance, Mahishasuramardini the most - the former for its
magnificence, the latter two for their intricate details</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left:36.0pt;text-indent:-18.0pt"><span lang="EN" style="font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:&quot;Calibri&quot;,sans-serif">-<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span><span lang="EN" style="font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:&quot;Calibri&quot;,sans-serif">Pancha
Rathas (20 minutes):</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left:72.0pt;text-indent:-18.0pt;border:none"><span lang="EN" style="font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:&quot;Calibri&quot;,sans-serif">○<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span><span lang="EN" style="font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:&quot;Calibri&quot;,sans-serif">This
is about 800 m away from the Lighthouse exit of the first complex</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left:72.0pt;text-indent:-18.0pt;border:none"><span lang="EN" style="font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:&quot;Calibri&quot;,sans-serif">○<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span><span lang="EN" style="font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:&quot;Calibri&quot;,sans-serif">It
has 5 beautiful <i>rathas</i> made of rock, named after the five Pandavas</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left:36.0pt;text-indent:-18.0pt"><span lang="EN" style="font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:&quot;Calibri&quot;,sans-serif">-<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span><span lang="EN" style="font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:&quot;Calibri&quot;,sans-serif">Shore
Temple (20 minutes):</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left:72.0pt;text-indent:-18.0pt;border:none"><span lang="EN" style="font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:&quot;Calibri&quot;,sans-serif">○<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span><span lang="EN" style="font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:&quot;Calibri&quot;,sans-serif">It’s
about 1.5 kms from the Pancha Rathas - so I took an auto-rickshaw</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left:72.0pt;text-indent:-18.0pt;border:none"><span lang="EN" style="font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:&quot;Calibri&quot;,sans-serif">○<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span><span lang="EN" style="font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:&quot;Calibri&quot;,sans-serif">It’s
a beautiful temple complex with lawns around it and the sea in front of it</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left:72.0pt;text-indent:-18.0pt;border:none"><span lang="EN" style="font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:&quot;Calibri&quot;,sans-serif">○<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span><span lang="EN" style="font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:&quot;Calibri&quot;,sans-serif">I
stayed here till the sunset</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left:36.0pt"><i><span lang="EN" style="font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:&quot;Calibri&quot;,sans-serif">(For
more information about the monuments, you can refer to the links posted at the
end)</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left:36.0pt;text-indent:-18.0pt"><span lang="EN" style="font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:&quot;Calibri&quot;,sans-serif">-<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span><span lang="EN" style="font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:&quot;Calibri&quot;,sans-serif">On
my way back, I came across a Kerala massage centre and decided to unwind after

<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left:36.0pt;text-indent:-18.0pt"><span lang="EN" style="font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:&quot;Calibri&quot;,sans-serif">-<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span><span lang="EN" style="font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:&quot;Calibri&quot;,sans-serif">Finally,
I returned to the hotel, took a shower and headed to the beach cafe of Sea
Breeze Resort for dinner, again with the same old company of my book</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left:72.0pt;text-indent:-18.0pt;border:none"><span lang="EN" style="font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:&quot;Calibri&quot;,sans-serif">○<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span><span lang="EN" style="font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:&quot;Calibri&quot;,sans-serif">It
was an average restaurant, with basic ambience, menu and service</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left:72.0pt;text-indent:-18.0pt;border:none"><span lang="EN" style="font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:&quot;Calibri&quot;,sans-serif">○<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span><span lang="EN" style="font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:&quot;Calibri&quot;,sans-serif">However,
the weather and the view made up for everything else :)</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal"><b><span lang="EN" style="font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;

<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left:36.0pt;text-indent:-18.0pt"><span lang="EN" style="font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:&quot;Calibri&quot;,sans-serif">-<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span><span lang="EN" style="font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:&quot;Calibri&quot;,sans-serif">Having

on the beach - around 9 am, I decided to go for a walk on the beach. I plugged
in my headphones and started walking north of my hotel</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left:72.0pt;text-indent:-18.0pt;border:none"><span lang="EN" style="font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:&quot;Calibri&quot;,sans-serif">○<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span><span lang="EN" style="font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:&quot;Calibri&quot;,sans-serif">Mornings
at the beach are amazing! The weather is ideal, wind is perfect, sand is soft

<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left:72.0pt;text-indent:-18.0pt;border:none"><span lang="EN" style="font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:&quot;Calibri&quot;,sans-serif">○<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span><span lang="EN" style="font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:&quot;Calibri&quot;,sans-serif">I
walked for about 1.5 kms, seeing on the way, other tourists calmly walking
along, happy kids playing in the water, some locals catching fish / shells,
beautiful resorts and a dead turtle washed ashore, all the while listening to
soothing music</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left:72.0pt;text-indent:-18.0pt;border:none"><span lang="EN" style="font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:&quot;Calibri&quot;,sans-serif">○<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span><span lang="EN" style="font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:&quot;Calibri&quot;,sans-serif">People

and figuring out things on one’s own is surprisingly refreshing. </span></p>
